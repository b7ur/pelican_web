Eat hungry

Zen Master Lingyou was preaching at the temple, the temple accountant was knocking on the wooden fish, and the chef suddenly clapped his hands and laughed. Zen Master Lingyou asked: “What are you doing?” The chef said, “I am hungry without eating, so I am happy.” Lingyou nodded. Eat hungry, such a simple reason is worth so happy? Yes, eat when you are hungry. Do not think too much
